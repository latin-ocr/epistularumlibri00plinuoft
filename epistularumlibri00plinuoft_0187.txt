1. studia. Doring is nearly right when he says that the
absolute use of studia in our modern sense of “studies '' is
confined to late Latinity. But such phrases as studiis ac
litteris (Cic. ad Att. viii. 11), studia exercere (ad Fam. ix. 8),
seri studiorum (Hor. Sat. i. 10, 12) show a near approach to
the later usage.
